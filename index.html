FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical website project take?
Project timelines depend on complexity. A standard WordPress or Shopify website typically takes 2–4 weeks. Larger, more complex projects can take 6–8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our discovery call so you know exactly what to expect.
What information do I need to provide to get started?
To kick things off, I'll need your business details, brand assets (logo, colors, fonts if available), content (or I can help with that), reference websites you like, and your project goals. We'll cover all of this in our initial consultation.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes, absolutely. Every project includes multiple rounds of revisions to ensure you're 100% satisfied with the result. I work closely with clients at every stage — from wireframes to final delivery — to make sure nothing gets missed.
How much does a website design cost?
Pricing varies based on the platform, scope, and complexity of your project. I offer packages to fit different budgets — from starter websites to enterprise-level builds. Contact me for a custom quote tailored to your specific needs.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Yes! I provide 30 days of post-launch support for all website projects to handle any bugs, tweaks, or questions. I also offer ongoing maintenance and support retainers for clients who need regular updates.
